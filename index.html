About Us

Learn more about CYBERHAX, our mission, and our vision for the future.

BACKGROUND:
CYBERHAX was founded in 2015 but was inactive from 2017 to 2023. Efforts to revive the club began in 2023.

MISSION & VISION:
To empower UTP students with essential computer skills, coding proficiency, and cybersecurity knowledge, fostering a collaborative learning environment and providing early industry exposure to prepare them for the challenges of the digital age.
